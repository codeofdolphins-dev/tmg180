Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to the "Find Workers" tab in your sidebar. You can use filters to find people based on location, availability, and specific skills that match your NDIS goals.

Once you find a suitable profile, click on "Contact" or "View Profile" and select the message icon. You can start a secure, encrypted conversation directly on the platform.

The worker will receive a notification and can choose to accept, decline, or message you back for more details. You can track all statuses in your "My Requests" tab.

Go to the "My Profile" tab in the bottom left of your sidebar. From there, you can edit your personal details, support preferences, and availability.
